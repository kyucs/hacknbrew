Hack & Brew 2024

Be a part of the first official KYUCSA event.

Come hang out with over 70+ fellow students and developers to talk shop, enjoy a few drinks, and get a preview of all the new stuff the KYUCSA team is working on.

Live from beautiful TTE 202 in our community of Kyambogo University, Hack & Brew is going to be the ultimate celebration of KYUCSA for anyone who's fallen in love with the journey to gaining a career in computer science.

Schedule

  • Welcome and Introductions

    5:30 PM - 6:15 PM

    Kick off the evening with a warm welcome! Grab a coffee and introduce yourself. Share your name, major, and any exciting topics/projects you're currently learning/working on.

  • Project Spotlight

    6:15 PM - 6:45 PM

    Join us for a lively sharing session where fellow students discuss their projects and tech interests. Each participant will have 5 minutes to share their insights, followed by a brief Q&A.

  • Group Discussion

    6:45 PM - 7:15 PM

    Join a small group to discuss current challenges, share insights, or explore topics like new technologies and coding practices. Let's brainstorm together!

  • Tech Talk/Workshop

    7:15 PM - 7:45 PM

    Stay tuned for an engaging talk or workshop led by a guest speaker or one of your peers on a relevant topic in computer science.

  • Networking & Wrap-Up

    7:45 PM - 8:00 PM

    Conclude the night with an open floor for any final thoughts or questions. Don't forget to exchange contact info to stay connected!

  • Evening Social

    8:00 PM - 9:00 PM

    Spend the rest of the evening making connections with other community members over snacks, and refreshments.
